Innovation

Converts your alphanumeric passwords into
complex hash-codes and ultimately into a color
sequence which is primarily your door key.
Now, all you got to do is show this color sequence
to the color scanning device on your door using a
mobile app. Once the pattern is recorded and
matched.....you get access !!
HURRAH !
