Innovation

Converts your alphanumeric passwords into
complex hash-codes and ultimately into a color
sequence which is primarily your door key.
Now, all you got to do is show this color sequence
to the color scanning device on your door using a
mobile app. Once the pattern is recorded and
matched.....you get access !!
HURRAH !

Many forms
One controller, many scanners.
One controller one scanner.
Unique user based multiple passwords.
Many uses
Can be a car lock or any lock you can imagine. Not just a lock though... can be used as a identification system as well.... You might see this instead of those biometric attendance systems someday.

Customers

The total addressable market (TAM) of our product consists of any and every individual and organization in need of security through identity verification.
• Our serviceable addressable market (SAM) would be individuals and organizations having access to internet and smart phones on a daily basis i.e.
people who have an average income level higher than 4 lac per annum.
• The serviceable addressable market consists of Individuals looking for efficient smart lock systems at an affordable price for residential
security- At a micro level
• Establishments and organizations (like restaurants, schools, corporate offices etc.) that require efficient and standard security.
